About Help Together Foundation

Mission and Background:

The foundation, named "Avbodh," derives its name from the Sanskrit word for "awareness." Avbodh was established with a noble mission: to disseminate knowledge and foster education, particularly among children in rural villages. The initiative recognizes that education is a powerful tool for personal and community transformation, and it focuses on bridging the educational divide that exists between urban and rural areas. Founded in 2004, Help Together Foundation Ltd. operates under Section 25 of the Indian Companies Act, which allows for the formation of non-profit entities dedicated to promoting charitable causes. The foundation’s core objective is to make education accessible to all, irrespective of geographic or socio-economic barriers. By focusing on villages that are often underserved, Avbodh aims to empower children with the skills and knowledge necessary to improve their futures and uplift their communities. Through its various programs, Avbodh provides educational resources, training, and support to both students and educators. These programs are designed not only to enhance academic skills but also to instill a sense of purpose and self-reliance. The foundation believes that by equipping children with education and critical thinking skills, it can help them break free from the cycle of poverty and contribute positively to society. In addition to its educational efforts, the foundation also emphasizes the importance of community involvement and partnership. By collaborating with local stakeholders and leveraging community resources, Avbodh strives to create sustainable and impactful educational initiatives. The foundation’s commitment to spreading awareness and knowledge reflects its broader goal of fostering social and economic development through education. Overall, Help Together Foundation Ltd. stands as a beacon of hope and progress, dedicated to making education a reality for all children, regardless of their background. Its work continues to make a significant difference in the lives of many, embodying the true spirit of service and dedication to societal betterment.

Key Figures:

Mr. Ravin Vyas, the director, collaborates with Ms. Hiral Mehta of the Innovative Quality Group Spirit Evolves Via Awareness (SEVA) project. Together, they launched the AIREP (Avbodh IQG Rural Empowerment Program), designed to create a self-sustainable model for rural empowerment through education, vocation, and healthcare.

Our Achievements

Here's a more detailed version: --- Over the past 18 months, the foundation’s initiatives have made a profound impact on villages surrounding Ahmedabad city. The Avbodh Rural Empowerment Program (AIREP) has been a cornerstone of this effort, aimed at transforming educational outcomes in these underserved communities. When the AIREP initiative was launched, it began with a modest cohort of just 10 students. These early participants were the first beneficiaries of the program’s comprehensive educational strategies, which include tailored teaching methods, resource distribution, and community engagement. Recognizing the initial success and the potential for broader impact, the program has grown exponentially, now serving over 300 regular attendees. This impressive expansion highlights the community’s increasing trust and engagement with the program. One of the most notable achievements of AIREP has been its effect on the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) exam results in Paldi Kankrej, a village where educational performance had previously been a major concern. Prior to AIREP's intervention, the pass rate for the SSC exams in Paldi Kankrej was alarmingly low, with less than 20% of students achieving passing grades. This low performance reflected a broader issue of inadequate educational resources and support. However, through targeted educational interventions, including supplementary classes, mentorship, and improved study materials, AIREP has played a pivotal role in reversing this trend. Within just one year, the pass rate in Paldi Kankrej has surged to over 60%. This dramatic improvement is a testament to the effectiveness of AIREP’s approach and the hard work of the students and educators involved. The success of the AIREP initiative not only demonstrates the foundation’s ability to create positive change but also serves as a model for similar efforts in other regions. By focusing on providing high-quality education and addressing specific local needs, Avbodh is making substantial strides in enhancing educational outcomes and empowering young learners in rural areas. The significant improvement in exam results is more than just a statistic; it represents a brighter future for many students and a step toward greater educational equity.
